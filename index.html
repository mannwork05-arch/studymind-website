Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 1, 2026

StudyMind is committed to protecting student privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: Name, email, class, school on registration.
  • Usage: Quiz scores, XP, study sessions for personalization.
  • Device: Android version for compatibility only.
  • Location: Only when YOU activate Safety Hub (SOS/Safe Walk). Never in background.
  • Camera/Mic: Only when you actively use Smart Scanner or Evidence Capture.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve educational services
  • To personalize your learning experience
  • To send Safety Hub SOS alerts to your trusted contacts (only when YOU activate)
  • To send OTP emails for password reset
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT display advertisements

3. Data Security

Data stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) on AWS ap-northeast-1. JWT authentication and HTTPS encryption for all transmissions. Row Level Security ensures only you access your data.

4. Safety Hub Data

Location data during SOS is used only to alert your trusted contacts. Evidence Capture data (photos, audio, location) is stored securely — accessible only to you.

5. Children's Privacy

StudyMind is for students 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Groq AI / Google Gemini: Processes AI Tutor queries.
  • Resend: OTP password reset emails only.
  • Railway: Backend server hosting.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data through your profile
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your data on request
